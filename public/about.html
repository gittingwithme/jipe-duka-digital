Our Story

A journey from the Kenyan highlands to your cup

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2009 in the heart of Nairobi, Kifaru Coffee began as a small family venture with a simple mission: to share the exceptional taste of Kenyan coffee with the world.

Our name "Kifaru" — meaning rhinoceros in Swahili — represents our commitment to strength, resilience, and protecting our heritage. Just as the rhino is an iconic symbol of Kenya's wildlife, we strive to be an iconic symbol of Kenyan coffee excellence.

Today, we work directly with over 200 smallholder farmers across the Central Highlands, ensuring fair wages, sustainable practices, and the highest quality beans from farm to cup.
